HI, I'M LIAM

A BIT ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Liam and I'm a Junior Software Engineer based in Manchester, UK.

After a number of years of running about dark studios, slipping on ice rinks and watching the 500th user submitted video of a baby falling over, I recently decided to call ‘Cut!’ on my TV production career and .push() myself into the world of Tech.

Whilst looking for a place to start, I was lucky enough to successfully apply for a free Pro membership on Codecademy during COVID and begin my first steps into the grand world of software development on the ‘Web Development Career Path’. From this point I truly found myself falling in love with coding, a subject I thought was well beyond my understanding. To further enhance my self-learning, I began absorbing every resource I could on software development in both book form and online resources.

After a few months of self-learning and creating projects I had only envisioned in my mind, I wanted to truly enhance my coding skills for both myself and potential future employers. From this point I enrolled in the coding bootcamp Northcoders.

I will be graduating from Northcoders in January 2021, and will come out of the other side with a range of full-stack development skills that I hope I can continue to expand upon within the foreseeable future!

EXPERIENCE

I've been coding since March 2020, making use of learning resources such as Codecademy and FreeCodeCamp, and also attending the Northcoders coding school in Manchester.

I'm always eager to learn new skills, and hope to positively contribute to a number of projects in this fast paced industry.
